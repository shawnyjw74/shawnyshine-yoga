🌿 Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
Stand tall, feet grounded, arms relaxed. Focus on your breath and posture—this pose builds awareness and balance.
🌬️ Box Breathing
Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat to calm your nervous system and reset your energy.

Start your day with 5 minutes of gentle movement—cat-cow, forward fold, and seated twist. It awakens your spine and spirit.
